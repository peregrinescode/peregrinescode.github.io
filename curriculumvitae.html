Research experience and skills

Topics: Molecular dopants, organic thin-film transistors and organic solar cells.

  • Vacuum deposition of thin-films and a broad range of organic devices including photovoltaic cells, light-emitting diodes and field-effect transistors.
  • Electrical, structural and photophysical characterisation techniques including conductivity, mobility, electron paramagnetic resonance, grazing incidence wide angle X-ray scattering, and absorption.
  • Maintenance and upgrades of the vacuum deposition system including software development for logging and automating standard procedures.
  • Numerical calculations solving Fermi-Dirac integrals in python. Example code can be found online at github.com/peregrinescode.
